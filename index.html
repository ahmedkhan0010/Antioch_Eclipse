Our Story

Born in the Shadow

The Antioch Eclipse is more than a cricket team — it's a movement. Founded with a vision to bring championship-calibre cricket to Antioch, we've built a family of dedicated athletes who play hard, train harder, and never stop competing.

Our mint-and-black colours stand for precision and power. Every time the Eclipse takes the field, we bring intensity, unity, and skill that sets us apart from every opponent.

Whether it's a fierce T20 or a tense limited-overs match, the Eclipse always shows up. Join us on this journey from underdogs to champions.
